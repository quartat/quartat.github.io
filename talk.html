The Talk

Services offered

Conversation and Communication skills

Good communication skills are the key to build a strong social network and the key to success in life, work and relationships. Without effective communication, a message can turn into blunder, misunderstanding, frustration, or even calamity by being misinterpreted or poorly delivered. To master the art of communication, we will inculcate basic conversation principles, the art of instant rapport, how to initiate and gracefully end the conversation, the art of small talk etiquette, what social networking dos and don’ts and how to be an effective communicator.


Dining Skills, Table Manners and Cocktail Party Etiquette

Understanding the art of Dinning and Entertaining finesse and why is it crucial to make the first impression and effectively build rapport across cultures. At Quartet Lifestyle you will learn to master your professional presence and enhance your confidence in understanding the styles of dining, formal table setting, silverware savvy, navigating the table place setting, the dinning dos and don’ts. It is not only how you place the food into your mouth but also, how you respond to conversations, what you wear and how you are seated that is considered essential in a fine dining setting. This program includes a fine dining experience.
