Manners

This is how we interact and communicate with one another; our manners say so much more about us than we realize. The institution of all manners is the fundamental needs of the human brain and how we best meet these needs through expressions of humanity, kindness, compassion and respect for self, others, property and the milieu.


Personal Style and Wardrobe Assessment

How to develop your own personal style, not by following fashion trends but by showcasing your best features to create the most flattering and timeless look to suite you. We will evaluate and identify the following: your body structure (shape of your face and body); what colours suites you, how to dress to achieve your goals, your business wardrobe, what are your hairstyle and makeup are conveying about you. Upon evaluation, we will provide you with guidance to your style, wardrobe, colours, makeup techniques, hairstyles and accessories to spotlight your features for all occasions
