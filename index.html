About Quartat

Success in business, personal relationships and in social situations demands confidence, self-awareness, good manners, style and unmatched charm.
Quartat Inc. is Toronto’s first multicultural institute dedicated to helping foreign students and international professionals learn the art of charm, etiquette and style both for personal and professional growth.
In today’s competitive job market, fast-paced networking events and online world of dating, it is simply not enough to have a top education or come from the right background.

Our soft skills are what set us apart from the competition. This includes hosting and table manners, business communication skills, dining etiquette, and the ability to dress impeccably to suit every occasion.
As Oscar Wilde once said, “Good manners opens doors that even good education cannot.”
We at Quartat believe our European training, countless interviews with experts and experience in the field can help the ordinary modern man or woman transform into the extraordinary global citizen they were meant to be; someone who embraces the culture they live in, and who treats others with respect and expects it in return.
